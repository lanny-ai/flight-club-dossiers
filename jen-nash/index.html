The Situation on the Ground
Jen Nash has built something rare: a genuinely differentiated financial coaching practice with serious credentials, a distinct brand identity, and three fully developed service lines. Her differentiator is real -- she blends behavioral science, neuroscience, social work, and hypnotherapy in a space dominated by spreadsheet evangelists and budget shame. She serves individuals, corporations, and underserved communities including neurodiverse clients, caregivers, and people with disabilities.
